PanRes (Pan Resistance) is a unified collection of genes conferring resistance to antibiotics, heavy metals, and biocides. We have aggregated unique gene sequences from multiple databases like ResFinder, CARD, MegaRes, AMRFinderPlus, ARGANNOT, BacMet, and functionally verified genes and unified the ontology.

Find detailed information about each PanRes gene: browse antibiotic classes, predicted antibiotic resistance phenotype(s) and ontology across different resources.
